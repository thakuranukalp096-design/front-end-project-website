7 Refreshing Drinks

Masala Chai

Ingredients: Tea leaves, milk, water, sugar, cardamom, ginger, cinnamon.

Steps:

  1. Boil water with cardamom, ginger, and cinnamon.
  2. Add tea leaves and simmer for 2 minutes.
  3. Add milk and sugar, boil till frothy.
  4. Strain and serve hot.

Mango Lassi

Ingredients: Ripe mango, yogurt, sugar, milk, cardamom.

Steps:

  1. Blend mango pulp with yogurt and sugar.
  2. Add milk to adjust consistency.
  3. Sprinkle cardamom powder on top.
  4. Serve chilled.

Fresh Lime Soda

Ingredients: Lime, soda water, sugar, salt, ice cubes.

Steps:

  1. Squeeze fresh lime juice into a glass.
  2. Add sugar and salt to taste.
  3. Pour soda water and mix gently.
  4. Add ice cubes and serve immediately.

Buttermilk (Chaas)

Ingredients: Yogurt, water, salt, roasted cumin powder, mint leaves.

Steps:

  1. Whisk yogurt with water until smooth.
  2. Add salt and roasted cumin powder.
  3. Garnish with chopped mint leaves.
  4. Serve chilled.

Hot Chocolate

Ingredients: Milk, cocoa powder, sugar, chocolate syrup, marshmallows.

Steps:

  1. Heat milk in a saucepan.
  2. Add cocoa powder and sugar, stir well.
  3. Pour into a cup and drizzle with chocolate syrup.
  4. Top with marshmallows and serve hot.
