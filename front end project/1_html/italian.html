Classic Italian Dishes

Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients: Spaghetti, eggs, pancetta, Parmesan cheese, black pepper.

Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta until crisp.
  3. Mix eggs and Parmesan, combine with hot spaghetti off the heat.
  4. Add pancetta, pepper, and serve immediately.

Margherita Pizza

Ingredients: Pizza dough, tomato sauce, mozzarella, fresh basil, olive oil.

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C.
  2. Spread tomato sauce over rolled dough.
  3. Add mozzarella and fresh basil.
  4. Bake 10-12 mins until crust is golden. Serve hot.

Lasagna

Ingredients: Lasagna sheets, minced meat, tomato sauce, béchamel, cheese.

Steps:

  1. Cook minced meat with tomato sauce.
  2. Layer pasta sheets, meat, béchamel, and cheese.
  3. Repeat layers, top with cheese.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 30-35 mins until golden. Serve warm.

Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients: Arborio rice, mushrooms, onion, garlic, vegetable broth, Parmesan.

Steps:

  1. Sauté onions and garlic in olive oil.
  2. Add mushrooms and cook till soft.
  3. Add rice and gradually add broth, stirring continuously.
  4. Finish with Parmesan and serve creamy.

Pesto Pasta

Ingredients: Pasta, basil pesto, pine nuts, Parmesan, olive oil.

Steps:

  1. Cook pasta until al dente.
  2. Mix with prepared basil pesto.
  3. Top with pine nuts and Parmesan.
  4. Serve warm with extra olive oil drizzle.
