5 Classic Indian Dishes

Chole Masala

Ingredients: Chickpeas, onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, coriander, cumin, chole masala powder, lemon, coriander leaves.

Steps:

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight.
  2. Pressure cook until soft.
  3. Sauté onions, ginger, garlic, tomatoes, and spices.
  4. Add chickpeas, simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander and lemon. Serve hot with bhature or rice.

Paneer Butter Masala

Ingredients: Paneer, tomatoes, onions, butter, cream, mild spices.

Steps:

  1. Cook onions and tomatoes and blend into a smooth puree.
  2. Simmer puree with butter, cream, and spices.
  3. Add paneer cubes and cook until coated.
  4. Serve hot with naan or steamed rice.

Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients: Basmati rice, mixed vegetables, fried onions, saffron, spices.

Steps:

  1. Cook rice until 70% done.
  2. Prepare spiced mixed vegetables.
  3. Layer rice and vegetables, sprinkle saffron milk.
  4. Seal and cook on low heat for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Serve with raita.

Masala Dosa

Ingredients: Rice, lentils, potato, onion, spices, coconut chutney, sambar.

Steps:

  1. Prepare and ferment dosa batter.
  2. Cook potato masala with spices.
  3. Pour batter on a hot tawa, cook until golden and crisp.
  4. Place potato filling inside and fold.
  5. Serve with coconut chutney and sambar.

Butter Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken, yogurt, spices, tomatoes, cream, butter.

Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices for 2 hours.
  2. Cook chicken until slightly charred.
  3. Prepare tomato-cream-butter sauce and simmer chicken in it.
  4. Cook until gravy thickens and chicken is tender.
  5. Serve with naan or jeera rice.
