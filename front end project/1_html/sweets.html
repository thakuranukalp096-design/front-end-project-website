Popular Sweets

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients: Khoya, flour, sugar, water, cardamom, rose water, oil/ghee for frying.

Steps:

  1. Mix khoya and flour to form a smooth dough.
  2. Shape into small balls and deep fry until golden brown.
  3. Prepare sugar syrup with cardamom and rose water.
  4. Soak fried balls in syrup for 1–2 hours before serving.

Jalebi

Ingredients: All-purpose flour, yogurt, sugar, water, saffron, ghee/oil for frying.

Steps:

  1. Prepare batter with flour and yogurt, ferment for a few hours.
  2. Pipe batter into hot oil in spiral shapes and fry until crisp.
  3. Soak fried jalebis in sugar syrup.
  4. Serve warm.

Rasgulla

Ingredients: Chhena (paneer), semolina, sugar, water, cardamom.

Steps:

  1. Prepare chhena and knead smooth with semolina.
  2. Shape into small balls.
  3. Boil balls in sugar syrup until soft and spongy.
  4. Cool and serve chilled.

Kaju Katli

Ingredients: Cashew nuts, sugar, water, cardamom powder, ghee.

Steps:

  1. Grind cashews to a fine powder.
  2. Boil sugar with water to make syrup.
  3. Add cashew powder, cook until thick.
  4. Roll dough, cut into diamond shapes after cooling.

Besan Laddu

Ingredients: Gram flour (besan), ghee, sugar, cardamom.

Steps:

  1. Roast besan in ghee until golden and fragrant.
  2. Add powdered sugar and cardamom.
  3. Mix well and shape into small round laddus.
  4. Cool and store in airtight container.
