Chinese Dishes

Vegetable Fried Rice

Ingredients: Rice, mixed vegetables, soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, spring onion.

Steps:

  1. Cook rice and let it cool.
  2. Stir-fry vegetables and garlic in sesame oil.
  3. Add rice and soy sauce, mix well.
  4. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.

Hakka Noodles

Ingredients: Noodles, carrots, capsicum, cabbage, soy sauce, garlic, chili sauce.

Steps:

  1. Boil noodles till al dente and drain.
  2. Stir-fry vegetables with garlic.
  3. Add noodles, soy sauce, and chili sauce.
  4. Toss well and serve hot.

Schezwan Noodles

Ingredients: Noodles, Schezwan sauce, mixed vegetables, garlic, soy sauce.

Steps:

  1. Boil noodles and drain.
  2. Stir-fry vegetables and garlic.
  3. Add Schezwan sauce and noodles, mix thoroughly.
  4. Serve hot garnished with spring onion.

Vegetable Manchurian

Ingredients: Cabbage, carrot, capsicum, cornflour, soy sauce, garlic, chili sauce.

Steps:

  1. Make vegetable balls with cornflour and spices.
  2. Fry until crisp.
  3. Prepare sauce with soy and chili sauce, garlic.
  4. Add balls to sauce and cook for 2 minutes. Serve hot.

Chicken Manchurian

Ingredients: Chicken, soy sauce, chili sauce, garlic, cornflour, onion, capsicum.

Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken with cornflour and spices.
  2. Fry chicken pieces until golden.
  3. Prepare sauce with soy, chili sauce, garlic.
  4. Add fried chicken to sauce, mix well, serve hot.
